# Echo Drift

Echo Drift is a Godot 4 2.5D exploration slice built as a stronger game development application piece.

## Pitch
The player crosses a suspended research ruin, recovering fragmented relic scans from a collapsed sky-platform. The project focuses on movement feel, scene composition, and atmospheric presentation over content sprawl.

## What This Shows

- Godot scene organization
- player movement and collision
- environmental composition
- collectible-driven progression
- game-ready presentation rather than a UI-only mockup

## Application Blurb

Built a Godot 4 exploration vertical slice centered on traversal, collectible scanning, and environmental storytelling. The project was scoped to demonstrate movement systems, readable level composition, and polished presentation in a compact portfolio-ready format.
