Why this sample fits

Designed to feel like a real game slice, not just a prototype stub.

Echo Drift was scoped as a focused suspended-ruin experience with traversal, collectible recovery, atmospheric scene composition, and clean first-play readability. The goal was to demonstrate game development judgment through finish, clarity, and production-minded scope.

Engine

Godot 4

Scene-driven implementation with movement, pickup logic, and presentational polish.

What it demonstrates

  • 2.5D exploration and player movement
  • Collectible-driven progression and scene feedback
  • Environmental composition and visual hierarchy
  • Polished portfolio framing for real-time interactive work
